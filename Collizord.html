About Us

Collizord is an AI-based accident detection and alert system designed to enhance road safety. It uses dashcam footage analyzed by a YOLO model, along with live location tracking and Twilio-powered emergency alerts. The system instantly notifies hospitals and contacts when a crash is detected. A force sensor module is planned as an add-on. Collizord targets car manufacturers, commercial fleets, and safety agencies with a subscription-based software model.

The Problem

Delayed Emergency Response:

No witnesses report the crash in most accidents. Victims remain unconscious for vital minutes or hours before rescue. Most hazardous when driving in rural roads or at night.

No Real-Time Location Sharing:

Emergency responders do not know the precise location of the crash. Valuable time is lost trying to track down the accident

Existing Systems Are Incomplete:

Dashcams can capture, but don't sense or alert. Post-accident solutions (such as Tractable) operate after the damage is already caused, not in real time.
